About this Blog

This blog name is inspired by the book Pale Blue Dot: A Vision of the Human Future in Space written by Carl Segan in 1997. It comes from the image of earth taken by Voyager 1 as it was about to leave our solar system. Our homeland is a mere point of light, a crescent only 0.12 pixel in size.

NASA / JPL

Look again at that dot. That’s here. That’s home. That’s us. On it everyone you love, everyone you know, everyone you ever heard of, every human being who ever was, lived out their lives. The aggregate of our joy and suffering, thousands of confident ideologies, economic doctrines, every hunter and forager, every hero and coward, every creator and destroyer of civilization, every king and peasant, every young couple in love, every mother and father, hopeful child, inventor and explorer, every teacher of morals, every corrupt politician, every “superstar,” every “supreme leader,” every saint and sinner in the history of our species lived there–on a mote of dust suspended in a sunbeam.

– Carl Sagan, Pale Blue Dot, 1994
